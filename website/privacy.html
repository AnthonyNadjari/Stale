Privacy Policy

Stale browser extension · Last updated: February 2025

Stale (“we”, “our”) respects your privacy. This policy describes how the Stale browser extension handles information.

Data we do not collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data, browsing history, or any identifiable information to our servers.

Data processed locally by the extension

The extension may use the following only on your device:

  • Storage — Your preferences (e.g. freshness thresholds, badge position, on/off state) and local quota/cache are stored in the browser’s extension storage. This data stays on your device.
  • Page and search results — To show “stale” indicators, the extension reads visible content and metadata (e.g. dates, meta tags) on web pages and search result pages. This processing is done locally; nothing is sent to us.
  • HTTP headers — For some pages, the extension may read the Last-Modified response header to infer freshness. This is used only locally to display a date and is not transmitted.

Third parties

If you purchase Stale Pro, payment is handled by the Chrome Web Store (or another payment provider we may use). Their respective privacy policies apply to that transaction. We do not receive or store your payment details.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will be revised when we do. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at the support or contact option provided where you obtained the extension (e.g. Chrome Web Store listing or stale-extension.com).
